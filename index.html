Every system I build follows a clean, structured process so you always know exactly what's happening and what to expect.

01
💬
Discovery Call
We get on a call and talk through your business, your biggest time-wasters, and what you wish happened automatically. No tech jargon, just honest conversation about what you actually need.
02
🗺️
System Design
Before writing a single workflow, I map out the full system architecture. You see exactly what will be built, how every piece connects, and what the end result looks like before work begins.

I build the system, test it with real data, and run it through every edge case. Nothing goes live until it works perfectly. You're updated throughout and can give feedback at every stage.
